This page describes how to request deletion of personal data collected by the mobile game Multi Ball: Tap & Evolve.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, follow these steps:

  1. Open your email client and create a new message.
  2. Send the email to:
    lesnikov1@icloud.com
  3. In the subject line, write: Multi Ball — Data Deletion Request
  4. In the body of the email, include:
    • Your Firebase User ID (if known — otherwise just describe your device)
    • The approximate date you started using the app
    • A brief statement: "I request deletion of all data associated with my use of Multi Ball: Tap & Evolve"
  5. Send the email.
  6. You will receive a confirmation reply within 7 days, and full deletion will be completed within 30 days.

Automatic Deletion

If you uninstall the app and do not use it for 90 days, the following data is automatically deleted:

  • Firebase Anonymous User ID
  • Leaderboard entry (if not in top 100)
  • Server logs and IP addresses
